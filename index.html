Did You Know?

The Rosary began in the Middle Ages as a simple way for ordinary believers to pray and reflect on the life of Christ. Tradition holds that the devotion was strongly promoted by Saint Dominic after receiving inspiration from Mary, mother of Jesus. Over time, the Rosary became one of the most loved Catholic prayers, guiding millions of faithful in meditation, peace, and devotion across cultures and generations.
